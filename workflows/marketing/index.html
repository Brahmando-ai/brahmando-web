Reach · Agentic marketing orchestrator

Local digital marketing for SMBs

Built for businesses that depend on local reach — Facebook, Instagram, neighborhood groups, and email — not enterprise ad stacks. Input: your website URL plus what's happening this week. Output: scheduled, owner-approved posts across channels.

Pilot: primovite.com — order-to-cash on Rhythm (online store) plus Reach for local campaigns.

Channels

  • Facebook & local groups

    Auto-suggest community groups from business address; owner confirms before posting.

  • Instagram & LinkedIn

    Channel-specific copy from one owner brief — promos, roadshows, hiring.

  • Email

    Newsletter and promo blasts to opted-in customer lists.

  • YouTube

    Publish marketing videos with SEO titles and local keywords.

  • Local portals

    Nextdoor, Google Business, chamber listings — surfaced by city and vertical.

Agentic flow

  1. 1Onboard business URL + address + owner WhatsApp/Telegram
  2. 2Crawl site for brand voice, services, and offers
  3. 3Owner sends briefs (roadshow, sale, new product) via chat or form
  4. 4Agent plans weekly calendar and drafts multi-channel posts
  5. 5Owner approves on WhatsApp — auto-publish via Rhythm/n8n schedulers
  6. 6Engagement feedback improves the next cycle

API (port 8220)

POST /businesses/onboard

POST /businesses/{id}/brief

POST /drafts/{id}/approve
