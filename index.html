Brahmexa, ManjuLAB & Brahmando

Brahmexa is the umbrella group brand. A portfolio of operating companies builds and delivers AI products and services under that brand, each with a distinct mandate and customer focus.

ManjuLAB is the customer-facing operating company within the group: it carries P&L responsibility, engages enterprise clients, and authors the agents, MCP servers, and workflows that appear in the Brahmando repository. Access to the full catalog — including deployment guides and integration support — is available through ManjuLAB commercial agreements or the community access program.

Brahmando is the Brahmexa group surface that hosts ManjuLAB-developed assets. Brahmando and ManjuLAB are peers within the group — not a parent and subsidiary. The repository is a curated, access-gated catalog, not an open public marketplace.
